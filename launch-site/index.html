How it works Install GitHub
macOS menu-bar dictation

Hold a key. Say the thought. Get text.

Murmur turns your voice into text in the app you are already using. Hold F5 for local raw transcription or F6 for LLM-polished writing, then Murmur auto-pastes at your cursor.
